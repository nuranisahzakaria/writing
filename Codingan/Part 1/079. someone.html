Sometimes beautiful people come into your life for only a reason of time simply to show you that good people exist.

They come to rewind you that love is worth fighting for, that there are people in this world who will see you for who you truly are. They come to teach you how to love yourself, to nudge you in the right direction.

And oftentimes, these people leave just as quickly they came.

Let them go with love.
They were never yours to keep.
